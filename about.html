Our Story

RupeesWise was born from a simple observation: while India's economy is growing, many people still find personal finance confusing and intimidating. Complex jargon, biased advice, and a lack of accessible tools create barriers for hardworking individuals trying to build a secure future. We wanted to change that. We started with a single, powerful idea: what if financial planning could be as simple as using a calculator? This idea grew into a mission to create a suite of the most intuitive, accurate, and user-friendly financial calculators designed specifically for the Indian context.
