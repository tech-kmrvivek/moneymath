How to Plan a ₹1 Crore Corpus with Just a ₹5,000/month SIP

Published on July 2, 2025 • 4 min read

Thinking a ₹1 crore portfolio is a goal reserved for high earners? Think again. Thanks to the power of compounding, even a modest Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) of ₹5,000 per month can grow into a substantial corpus. Let's break down how long it would take.

Time Required to Reach ₹1 Crore

The time it takes to reach your goal depends heavily on the annual rate of return (CAGR) your investments generate. Here’s a look at the numbers:

Expected Return (CAGR) Years to ₹1 Cr Total Amount Invested Total Wealth Gained
10% ~26 years ₹15.6 Lakhs ₹84.4 Lakhs
12% ~23 years, 10 months ₹14.3 Lakhs ₹85.7 Lakhs
14% ~21 years, 8 months ₹13 Lakhs ₹87 Lakhs

As you can see, a mere 2% increase in returns can shorten your investment journey by several years. For long-term goals, diversified equity mutual funds have historically provided returns in the 12-15% range.

Want to Get There Even Faster? Use a Step-Up SIP

The most powerful tool to accelerate your wealth creation is the "Step-Up SIP" — increasing your monthly investment amount annually.

With a standard ₹5,000 SIP at 12%, it takes nearly 24 years to reach ₹1 crore.
But with a 10% annual step-up, you can reach the same goal in just ~18 years and 9 months!

Final Thoughts

Building significant wealth doesn't always require a large starting capital. What it absolutely requires is discipline, patience, and a long-term perspective. By starting a small SIP today and committing to increasing it over time, you can turn a distant dream of financial independence into an achievable reality.
