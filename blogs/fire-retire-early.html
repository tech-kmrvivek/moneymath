What is FIRE?

FIRE is a lifestyle movement centered around aggressive saving and investing. The goal is to accumulate enough income-generating assets so that your passive income can cover all your living expenses. Once you hit that point, you are financially independent and can "retire" from mandatory work.

The Core Principles of FIRE

  • High Savings Rate: This is non-negotiable. Many FIRE followers save 50-70% of their income. This requires a conscious effort to increase income and optimize expenses.
  • Frugal but Mindful Living: It's not about deprivation, but about spending money on what truly adds value to your life and cutting out the rest.
  • Smart Investing: The saved money must be put to work. Most followers use low-cost index funds and mutual funds to build wealth through compounding.

Key Challenges to Consider

  • Inflation: Your retirement corpus must be able to withstand rising costs over several decades.
  • Healthcare Costs: Without an employer, you'll need to budget for your own health insurance, which can be a significant expense.
  • Market Volatility: A major market crash early in your retirement can impact the longevity of your portfolio.
