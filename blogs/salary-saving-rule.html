💼 How Much Should I Save From My Salary? A Simple Rule

Published on July 2, 2025 • 4 min read

Most people struggle to decide how much to spend, save, and invest from their monthly salary. The good news? There’s a proven and simple budgeting formula that works for nearly everyone — the 50-30-20 Rule.

📊 What is the 50/30/20 Rule?

It’s a simple way to divide your monthly income into 3 buckets:

  • 50% for essentials (rent, groceries, bills)
  • 30% for lifestyle (shopping, travel, entertainment)
  • 20% for savings and investments

📌 Example: ₹60,000 Monthly Salary

Essentials (50%)

₹30,000

Lifestyle (30%)

₹18,000

Savings (20%)

₹12,000

💡 Tip: Try to grow your savings % over time, especially if expenses are low.

📈 What Happens If You Save 20% Consistently?

Let’s say you save ₹10,000 every month and invest it at 12% CAGR:

  • In 10 years → ₹23.2 Lakhs
  • In 15 years → ₹48.6 Lakhs
  • In 20 years → ₹98.4 Lakhs

Small monthly savings can lead to big wealth. Start early!

📌 Final Thoughts

The 50-30-20 rule is a great starting point for salary-based budgeting. As your income grows, increase your savings rate and prioritize long-term investments like SIPs or retirement funds. Your future self will thank you!
